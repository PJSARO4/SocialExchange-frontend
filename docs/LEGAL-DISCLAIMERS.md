## 1. E-Shares: NOT Securities (Important)

### Designation as Social Credits / Brand Points

E-Shares tokens on the Social Exchange are designated and operated as **"Social Credits"** or **"Brand Points"** — NOT as securities, investment contracts, or financial instruments.

**Key Points:**
- E-Shares are branded loyalty/engagement tokens tied to creator brands
- They represent **speculative brand engagement metrics**, not ownership or profit rights
- No dividend, distribution, or profit-sharing rights are attached
- E-Shares are **entertainment/gamification tokens only**
- Creators do NOT grant voting rights, ownership, or financial control through E-Shares
- Price fluctuations are based on algorithmic AMM pricing, NOT underlying business value

### Why This Distinction Matters

Under securities law (SEC regulations in the US, FCA regulations in the UK, etc.), if tokens:
- Represent ownership in an enterprise
- Provide expectation of profits
- Show financial performance metrics
- Allow voting or governance

...they are likely securities and subject to rigorous compliance requirements.

By operating E-Shares as **Social Credits without economic substance**, we avoid securities regulation — BUT this only works if:
1. We don't market them as investments
2. We don't promise returns or profits
3. We don't tie them to business metrics (revenue, subscribers, etc.)
4. We clearly state they are gamification tokens

### Recommended User Warnings

Display prominently on all E-Shares trading pages:

```
⚠️  E-Shares are Social Credits/Brand Points, NOT investments.
- No underlying asset value
- No guaranteed returns
- Prices set by supply/demand, not business performance
- Total loss of value is possible
- For entertainment purposes only
```

## 2. Trading Post: Account Transfer Marketplace

### No Guarantee of Account Transferability

The Trading Post facilitates the purchase and sale of social media accounts (Instagram, TikTok, etc.) between users. **Social media platform terms of service typically FORBID account transfers.**

### Required Disclaimer

Add to all Trading Post listings and confirmations:

```
⚠️  IMPORTANT: Transferring social media accounts may violate platform ToS.
- Instagram/TikTok/YouTube are owned by their respective platforms
- You may not own or sell accounts per platform terms of service
- Buyer and seller acknowledge and assume all legal risk
- Social Exchange is not liable for account suspension or reclamation
- Perform due diligence before purchasing
```

### Escrow as Risk Mitigation (NOT Guarantee)

The 7-stage escrow system (payment → funds held → credentials sent → verification → 48h lock → completion) provides:
- ✅ Reduces fraud between strangers
- ✅ Creates transaction history for dispute resolution
- ❌ Does NOT guarantee the account will remain accessible
- ❌ Does NOT guarantee platform won't ban/suspend the account
- ❌ Does NOT guarantee the seller has legal authority to transfer

### Seller Liability Notice

Sellers must acknowledge:

```
I represent and warrant:
1. I have legal authority to transfer this account
2. This account has not been purchased from another seller
3. I will comply with the platform's terms of service
4. I am not violating platform policies by selling this account
5. I accept liability if the account is suspended/reclaimed after sale
```

## 4. AMM Pricing & Slippage

### Automated Market Maker Notice

E-Shares use an automated pricing algorithm:

```
Pricing Formula: x * y = k (constant product)

This means:
- Large trades have high slippage (price impact)
- Prices move 1% per standard trade
- You may receive fewer shares/coins than expected
- Check quote before confirming order
- Max slippage protection available for limit orders
```

### Fee Structure

Clearly disclose all fees:

```
E-Share Trading Fees:
- Platform Fee: 1% of transaction
- Creator Royalty: 2% of transaction
- Total: 3% added to buy orders, deducted from sell orders

Example:
- Buy 100 shares at $1 = $100 cost
- With fees: $103 total deducted from wallet
- Creator receives $2 in royalties
- Platform receives $1 in fees
```

## 7. Regulatory Compliance Checklist

Before public launch, address:

- [ ] **Securities Law**: Consult attorney on E-Shares classification (likely securities in US)
- [ ] **Money Transmission**: Confirm if wallet/deposits require money transmitter license
- [ ] **Consumer Protection**: FTC guidelines for marketplace, fraud prevention
- [ ] **Gambling Laws**: Check if E-Shares trading is classified as gambling in your jurisdiction
- [ ] **Tax Compliance**: Report user trading activity (1099-B equivalent)
- [ ] **Privacy Law**: GDPR compliance if users are EU residents
- [ ] **Platform ToS**: Confirm Instagram/TikTok ToS allows account trading (likely NO)
- [ ] **Insurance**: Errors & Omissions (E&O) and Cyber Liability insurance
